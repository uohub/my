Case Study

Empowering Access to Fresh Produce

Problem

A sustainable food startup wanted to connect local farmers directly with urban consumers but struggled to communicate its mission clearly. Their messaging lacked emotional appeal, and website traffic wasn't converting into signups. The challenge was to craft copy that balanced authenticity, trust, and urgency—turning awareness into meaningful action. I redefined their brand voice around freshness, community, and purpose. Through SEO-driven web copy, compelling product descriptions, and story-led email campaigns, I built a consistent narrative that celebrated both farmers and customers. Within three months, engagement rose by 65%, and online orders doubled, strengthening their market presence and impact.
